Space CoBot is an aerial vehicle with hexrotor structure designed for
collaborative tasks inside space stations. This video presents
illustrative simulation results performed using the Space CoBot model
and controller presented in the associated paper. The simulations were
obtained using the V-REP robotics simulator with zero gravity
(g=0). The simulations consist of several trajectory tracking
examples, including a case of a un-modelled load transportation and a
case of multi-vehicle docking.
