Video submission corresponding to the following paper:
Pedro Roque and Rodrigo Ventura, "Space CoBot: modular
design of an holonomic aerial robot for indoor microgravity
environments", IROS, 2016.

This video should  play in any MPEG4 compliant multimedia player,
e.g., VLC, mplayer, etc. The codecs employed are H.264, AAC, AC3.
